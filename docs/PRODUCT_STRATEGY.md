# CineBlend Product Strategy

## Core Product Vision
CineBlend is an emotional, personal, and creative movie curation platform. It solves the "what to watch" problem by relying on human-curated, mood-based playlists rather than algorithmic recommendations. Users can share their movie taste authentically without showing off, and find films based on highly specific vibes and life moments.

## Target Audience
- Passionate cinephiles and movie lovers
- Casual viewers looking for specific mood-based recommendations
- Film communities and friend groups
- Users fatigued by generic AI or algorithmic suggestions

## Value Proposition
- **For Curators:** A beautiful, aesthetic platform to catalog and share movies they love with personal notes attached to each film.
- **For Viewers:** High-trust, human-curated movie lists that fit exact moods (e.g., "Rainy night comfort films" or "Mind-bending movies").

## Feature Breakdown

### MVP (Current Implementation)
1. **Authentication:** Secure Email/Password login powered by Supabase.
2. **User Profiles:** Auto-generated profiles with usernames and avatars.
3. **Playlist Creation:** Create public/private playlists with titles, descriptions, tags, and cover images.
4. **Movie Integration:** Search and add movies using the TMDb API, attaching a custom personal note to each film.
5. **Discovery Feed:** An Explore page featuring the latest community playlists.
6. **Social Sharing:** Open Graph metadata for rich previews on WhatsApp, X (Twitter), and Instagram DMs.

### Phase 2 (Advanced Features)
- **Collaborative Playlists:** Allow friends to build a playlist together.
- **AI Playlist Generation:** "Generate a playlist based on my prompt" using AI, but curated by humans.
- **Watch Status & Tracking:** Mark movies as Watched, Watching, or Want to Watch directly from playlists.
- **Social Engagement:** Comments on playlists, follow system for favorite curators, and weekly trending boards.

## Landing Page Copywriting
- **Headline:** Curate Your Cinematic Mood.
- **Sub-headline:** Build, share, and discover beautiful movie playlists. Step away from the algorithm and trust human taste.
- **Call to Action:** Start Curating for Free.

## Monetization Ideas
1. **CineBlend Pro ($5/month):**
   - Unlimited playlists (Free tier capped at 10).
   - Custom animated profile avatars and premium badges.
   - Advanced analytics (who viewed your playlist, demographics).
2. **Affiliate Links:**
   - "Where to watch" buttons (Amazon Prime, Apple TV) that generate affiliate revenue when users rent/buy the movie.
3. **Sponsored Playlists:**
   - Partner with indie studios or streaming services to feature curated lists ("A24's Best Slow Burns").
